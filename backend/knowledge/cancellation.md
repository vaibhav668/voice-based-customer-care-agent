# Cancellation Policy

## Standard Cancellation Rules
- Cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure: Full refund (100%).
- Cancellations made 12 to 24 hours before departure: 75% refund (25% cancellation fee).
- Cancellations made 4 to 12 hours before departure: 50% refund (50% cancellation fee).
- Cancellations made less than 4 hours before departure: No refund.
- After bus departure: No refund or cancellation is allowed.

## How to Cancel Your Booking
1. Log in to your account.
2. Go to "My Bookings".
3. Select the booking you want to cancel.
4. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm.
Alternatively, contact customer support with your booking code and they will initiate cancellation on your behalf.

## Cancellation via Customer Support (This Chat)
You can request cancellation through this chat. The agent will:
1. Confirm your booking details.
2. Check your cancellation eligibility.
3. Ask for your explicit confirmation before cancelling.
4. Once confirmed, the cancellation will be processed immediately.

## After Cancellation
Once a booking is cancelled:
- The status will change to CANCELLED.
- A refund will be initiated to the original payment method within 5-7 working days.
- A cancellation confirmation will be sent to your registered email.

## Non-Cancellable Bookings
- Bookings for special routes, charter services, or promotional fares may be non-refundable.
- Bookings cancelled after the bus has departed are non-refundable.

## Rescheduling vs. Cancellation
Rescheduling is available as an alternative to cancellation. Please refer to the rescheduling policy for more information.
