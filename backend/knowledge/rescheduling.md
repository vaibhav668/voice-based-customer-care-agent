# Rescheduling Policy

## Is Rescheduling Available?
Yes. Bookings can be rescheduled to a different date or time on the same route, subject to seat availability.

## Rescheduling Eligibility
- Rescheduling is allowed up to 6 hours before the original departure time.
- Only one reschedule is allowed per booking.
- Bookings with CANCELLED or COMPLETED status cannot be rescheduled.

## Rescheduling Fee
- Rescheduling more than 24 hours before departure: ₹50 rescheduling fee.
- Rescheduling 6 to 24 hours before departure: ₹100 rescheduling fee.
- Rescheduling less than 6 hours before departure: Not allowed (cancellation only).

## How to Reschedule
1. Contact customer support with your booking code and preferred new travel date.
2. Our agent will check seat availability on the requested date.
3. You will be informed of the rescheduling fee.
4. Confirm your request, pay the fee, and receive the updated ticket.

## Seat Availability
Rescheduling is subject to seat availability on the new date. If no seats are available, you may choose a different date or opt for cancellation with a refund.

## Price Difference
If the fare for the new date is higher, you will be required to pay the difference. If the fare is lower, no refund will be issued for the difference.

## Not Eligible for Rescheduling
- Already cancelled bookings.
- Bookings for travel that has already occurred.
- Special promotional bookings (subject to fare terms).
- Last-minute bookings (within 6 hours of departure).
