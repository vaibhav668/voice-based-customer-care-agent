# Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change my seat?
A: Seat changes are available subject to seat availability on the same bus. Please contact customer support with your booking code to request a seat change.

Q: Can I travel without an ID?
A: No. A valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhar card, PAN card, passport, or driving licence) is mandatory for boarding. Passengers without valid ID may be denied entry.

Q: Can I carry medicines?
A: Yes, personal medicines are allowed. Please carry the prescription if carrying prescription medication.

Q: Do buses have WiFi?
A: Select premium AC Sleeper buses offer WiFi. Please check your booking confirmation for onboard amenities.

Q: Is food served on the bus?
A: Food is not served on the bus. However, the bus makes scheduled stops at restaurants and dhabas during long journeys. You may carry your own dry snacks and water.

Q: Can I carry water on the bus?
A: Yes, passengers may carry personal water bottles and dry snacks.

Q: Are smoking and alcohol allowed?
A: No. Smoking and alcohol consumption are strictly prohibited on all buses.

Q: What if I miss my bus?
A: If you miss your bus, please contact customer support immediately. Missed bus tickets are generally non-refundable but may be rescheduled subject to the rescheduling policy.

Q: How early should I reach the boarding point?
A: Passengers should arrive at the boarding point at least 15 minutes before departure. The bus will not wait for late passengers.

Q: What is boarding point?
A: The boarding point is the specific pickup location for your journey. It is mentioned in your booking confirmation ticket. Please verify the boarding point before departure.

Q: What is drop point?
A: The drop point is where the bus will drop you at the destination city. It is mentioned in your ticket.

Q: Can I travel with a child?
A: Yes. Children above 5 years of age require a separate ticket. Children below 5 years of age can travel on the same seat as a parent at no extra charge.

Q: Is there a toilet on the bus?
A: Premium AC Sleeper buses are equipped with washrooms. Non-AC and economy buses may not have onboard restrooms but make scheduled stops.

Q: My ticket shows a wrong name. What do I do?
A: Please contact customer support immediately with your booking code. Name corrections can be made up to 2 hours before departure.

Q: How do I get a duplicate ticket?
A: You can retrieve your ticket at any time by logging in to your account and viewing your booking. The ticket will be available in your booking history.

Q: What documents are required for boarding?
A: You need a valid government ID and your ticket (either digital or printed). The booking confirmation code is sufficient in most cases.

Q: Can I board from a different point than mentioned on my ticket?
A: No. You must board from the boarding point mentioned on your ticket.
