# Payment Issues and Failed Payments

## Payment Failed — What to Do
If your payment failed:
1. Check your bank account or wallet to confirm whether the amount was debited.
2. If the amount was NOT debited, simply try booking again with the same or a different payment method.
3. If the amount WAS debited but the ticket was not confirmed, please follow the steps below.

## Money Deducted but Ticket Not Confirmed
This situation can occur due to a network interruption during payment. Here is what to do:
1. Wait 30 minutes. In most cases the payment gateway reverses the charge automatically.
2. Check your email for a booking confirmation. Sometimes the booking succeeds even if the app shows an error.
3. If no confirmation arrives and the amount is still debited after 30 minutes, contact customer support immediately with:
   - Your registered email address
   - The approximate amount debited
   - The payment method used
   - The date and time of the attempted transaction
4. Our team will verify with the payment gateway and either confirm your booking or initiate a full refund.

## Duplicate Payment
If you were charged twice for the same booking:
1. Do not make another booking attempt.
2. Contact customer support with both transaction IDs (from your bank statement or UPI app).
3. The duplicate charge will be refunded within 3-5 working days.

## Pending Payment Status
A PENDING payment status means the payment gateway has received your payment but has not yet confirmed it to our system. This is usually resolved within 15 to 30 minutes. Do not retry the payment. If the status remains PENDING after 1 hour, contact customer support.

## Payment Declined
Payments can be declined due to:
- Insufficient funds.
- Card limit exceeded.
- Bank security block on online transactions.
- UPI daily limit exceeded.
Please try a different payment method or contact your bank.

## Booking Not Found After Payment
If you completed payment but cannot find your booking in "My Bookings":
1. Wait 10 minutes and refresh.
2. Check your registered email for a confirmation.
3. Contact customer support with your transaction reference number.
