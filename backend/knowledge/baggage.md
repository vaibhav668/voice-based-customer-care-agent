# Baggage and Luggage Policy

## Standard Luggage Allowance
Every passenger is entitled to carry up to 20 kg of check-in luggage free of charge. This luggage is stored in the undercarriage storage compartment of the bus.

## Hand Luggage
Passengers may carry one small bag or backpack (up to 5 kg) as cabin luggage, to be stored in the overhead storage rack or under the seat.

## Extra Luggage
Luggage exceeding 20 kg will be charged at ₹30 per additional kilogram. Payment must be made at the boarding point before departure.

## Oversized Items
Bicycles, large musical instruments, and other oversized items cannot be accommodated on the bus. Please contact customer support 48 hours in advance for special arrangements.

## Two-Wheelers and Vehicles
Motorcycles, scooters, and other vehicles are not permitted on passenger buses.

## Prohibited Items
The following items are strictly prohibited:
- Explosives and flammable materials (fuel, crackers, etc.)
- Weapons, sharp objects, and knives
- Illegal narcotics and controlled substances
- Corrosive or hazardous chemicals
- Live animals (except as noted below)

## Pets
Pets are not allowed on the bus unless specifically approved by the transport authority. Only small pets in secure, leak-proof carriers may be permitted on selected routes with advance booking approval.

## Lost Luggage
If luggage is lost or misplaced, please report immediately to customer support with your booking code and a description of the lost item. We will trace the luggage and coordinate delivery. Provide a written complaint at the destination depot for insurance purposes.

## Medicines and Personal Items
Personal medicines, baby food, and essential personal items are always allowed regardless of size or weight restrictions.

## Liability
The bus operator is not liable for fragile, perishable, or valuable items carried in luggage. Passengers are advised not to leave valuables in luggage stored in the undercarriage.
