# Refund Policy

## Refund Processing Time
Refunds are typically processed within 5 to 7 working days after cancellation is confirmed.

## Refund to Original Payment Method
All refunds are credited back to the original payment method used at the time of booking:
- UPI payments: refunded to the same UPI ID within 5 business days.
- Debit/Credit Card: refunded to the same card within 7 business days.
- Net banking: refunded to the same bank account within 5-7 business days.
- Wallet payments: refunded to the same wallet within 2-3 business days.

## Refund Amount
The refund amount depends on when the cancellation was made (see Cancellation Policy):
- More than 24 hours before departure: 100% refund.
- 12 to 24 hours before departure: 75% refund.
- 4 to 12 hours before departure: 50% refund.
- Less than 4 hours or after departure: No refund.

## Checking Refund Status
You can check your refund status by:
1. Asking the customer support chatbot with your booking code.
2. Checking with your bank or payment provider.
If the refund does not arrive within 7 business days, please contact customer support.

## Failed or Missing Refund
If your refund has not been received after 7 working days:
1. Contact your bank or payment provider to check for pending credits.
2. Contact customer support with your booking code and cancellation date.
3. Provide your payment transaction ID if available.

## Dispute Resolution
If a refund is disputed, please contact customer support within 30 days of cancellation with relevant payment proof. Our team will investigate and respond within 3 business days.
